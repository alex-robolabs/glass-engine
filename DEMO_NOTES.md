# Demo notes for the instructor

**Show this first:** PREDICT on the projector. Tap "the robot", drag temperature to 0.2, hit Auto-finish twice (same safe loop both times), then drag to 2.0 and Auto-finish again. The room sees "randomness" become a dial in fifteen seconds, and the bars reweight live as you drag.

**The stage moment:** have the agent add a third preset to `ATTENTION_PRESETS` (search the file for CLASS SENTENCE SLOT) live, reload, and it simply appears as a new chip. Let a student pick the word before you reveal the routing.

**Avoid doing live:** freestyle word math with random dropdown words. The three preset chips are guaranteed to land (queen, drone, cat); arbitrary A - B + C combos land on the honest nearest neighbor, which is sometimes a shrug, not a punchline. Stick to the chips on stage and let the kids freestyle on their own phones.
