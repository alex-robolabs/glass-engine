MODULE 02The engine eats numbers

The engine never sees your letters, only numbered chunks called tokens. Type and watch the chopper work.

your text

See it? The model gets straw and berry, two chunks with ID numbers. It never sees the letters inside, which is why counting the r's in strawberry is famously hard.

Real tokenizers learn their chunk list and ID numbers from mountains of text. These ones are hand-made approximations, but the lesson is exact: the model reads numbers, never letters.
